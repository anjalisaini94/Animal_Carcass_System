About Us

Welcome to the innovative and compassionate world of Moradabad Smart City's Animal Carcass System! Our city has taken a significant leap towards providing a seamless solution for the respectful disposal of animal carcasses. We understand the deep bond between humans and animals, and to ensure their dignity even in their final moments, we have designed a user-friendly system that enables residents to fill a simple complaint form. Once submitted, our dedicated team of animal pickers swiftly respond to these requests, ensuring timely and efficient removal of the carcass. This groundbreaking initiative showcases our commitment to creating a city that not only embraces cutting-edge technology but also values the welfare and harmony between humans and animals. Together, let's build a smarter, more compassionate Moradabad!
