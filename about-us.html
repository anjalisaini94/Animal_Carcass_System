Moradabad Smart City

Moradabad.

Moradabad is situated on the banks of the Ramganga river, at a distance of 167 km (104 mi) from the national capital, New Delhi and 344 km north-west of the state capital Lucknow. The city is known as Pital Nagri, (“Brass City”) for its famous brass handicrafts industry.

Animal Carcass System

About the Project

Welcome to the innovative and compassionate world of Moradabad Smart City's Animal Carcass System! Our city has taken a significant leap towards providing a seamless solution for the respectful disposal of animal carcasses. We understand the deep bond between humans and animals, and to ensure their dignity even in their final moments, we have designed a user-friendly system that enables residents to fill a simple complaint form. Once submitted, our dedicated team of animal pickers swiftly respond to these requests, ensuring timely and efficient removal of the carcass. This groundbreaking initiative showcases our commitment to creating a city that not only embraces cutting-edge technology but also values the welfare and harmony between humans and animals. Together, let's build a smarter, more compassionate Moradabad!



Vision

The vision of the Moradabad Smart City's Animal Carcass System is to create a compassionate and efficient city where animal welfare is a priority. Through technology, we aim to promptly respond to complaints about animal carcasses, ensuring respectful disposal. Beyond just removal, we strive to foster a greater understanding and appreciation for animals, encouraging responsible pet ownership and coexistence with wildlife. Our ultimate goal is to set an example for other communities, showcasing that a smart city is one that embraces sustainability, compassion, and the well-being of all living beings. Together, we can build a brighter and more harmonious future for Moradabad and its inhabitants, humans, and animals alike.



Benefits

The implementation of the Moradabad Smart City's Animal Carcass System will bring significant change and benefits to the city. It will ensure prompt and respectful disposal of animal carcasses, enhancing cleanliness and hygiene in public spaces. By promoting responsible pet ownership and wildlife coexistence, the system will reduce animal-related issues and foster a harmonious urban environment. Moreover, the efficient use of technology will optimize response times, leading to a more compassionate and caring city for both residents and animals, setting an example for other communities to follow.
